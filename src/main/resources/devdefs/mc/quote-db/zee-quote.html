About Zee Entertain

Zee Entertain is in the Media & Entertainment sector. The current market capitalisation stands at Rs 51,273.55 crore.

The company management includes Subhash Chandra - Non Executive Chairman, Punit Goenka - Managing Director & CEO, Subodh Kumar - Non Executive Director, Ashok Kurien - Non Executive Director, Adesh Kumar Gupta - Independent Director, Sunil Sharma - Independent Director, Neharika Vohra - Independent Director, Manish Chokhani - Independent Director.

It is listed on the BSE with a BSE Code of 505537 and the NSE with an NSE Code of ZEEL.

Its Registered office is at 18th Floor, A Wing, Marathon Futurex,,N M Joshi Marg, Mumbai,Maharashtra - 400013.

Their Registrars are Link Intime India Pvt. Ltd.
